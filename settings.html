Twitch Notepad Help

This extension saves messages for each twitch channel in order to send it easily
  • Once you have added a message, type its title in the twitch chat and hit the "CTRL" button to automatically send the content of the saved message
  • When you are in this popup you can also mouseover the messages to see their content. If you click on the message it will also be send automatically
  • When a message is send through the extension it is also copied in your clipboard
If your message isn't automatically send or isn't copied in the twitch chat, try to reload your page.
